### Key Contributions

- **Multi-marginal Optimal Transport**: Extension of classical optimal transport to multiple marginals for temporal consistency
- **Schrödinger Bridge Framework**: Application of diffusion-based bridges for video generation
- **Static-to-Dynamic Translation**: Novel approach to generate videos from unpaired static images
- **Temporal Coherence**: Maintaining consistency across video frames through optimal transport

---

### Technical Approach

The method leverages **multi-marginal optimal transport** theory combined with **Schrödinger bridge** formulations to:

1. **Bridge Static Distributions**: Connect multiple static image distributions through optimal transport plans
2. **Temporal Interpolation**: Generate smooth temporal transitions using diffusion processes
3. **Unpaired Learning**: Train on static images without requiring paired video data
4. **Dynamic Synthesis**: Produce temporally coherent video sequences
